About

My name is Anh Ngo. I'm an aspiring UX/UI and visual designer based in Helsinki, Finland, originally from Vietnam.

I craft digital platforms, visual identities, and publications. I am interested in interaction design, interactive media, creative coding, visual systems, participatory design, and critical making.

When I’m not designing, I’m mending and researching textile repair, in collaboration with grassroot organisations. I also enjoy cooking, bouldering, and hiking.
