Anh Ngo

I am a designer dedicated to creating meaningful digital and analog experiences (+)(-)

I craft web platforms, visual identities, publications, and brand images. I’m also interested in media and designs that explore alternative ways of connecting and interacting. I’m equipped with graphic design and UX/UI skills, and an eye for visual systems.

I was born and raised in Vietnam. I am currently based in Helsinki, Finland, where I obtained a Bachelor of Arts in Design from Aalto University. When I’m not designing, I’m mending and researching textile repair, in collaboration with grassroot organisations.
